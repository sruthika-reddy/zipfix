Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes! You can cancel your subscription anytime from your profile. Your plan remains active until the end of the billing period.
How does the discount work? ▾
Your plan discount is automatically applied at checkout when you book any service. No coupon codes needed.
What if I use more than my plan limit? ▾
For Basic (2/month) and Standard (5/month), additional services beyond your limit are charged at the regular rate with no discount.
How do I pay for quarterly plans? ▾
Quarterly plans are a one-time payment for 3 months. You save 25% compared to monthly billing.
